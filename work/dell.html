My Role

Product Designer

Description

Created a modular, customizable design system for Wokiva to use in their products with the flexibility for their own clients to theme it for their individual brand identities.

Context

Workiva is a cloud-based compliance and reporting platform for risk, accounting, finance, and compliance users. They came to Funsize seeking to modernize the design of their product to stand out from their competitors.

Concept Design

As part of the refresh of their design, Workiva wanted a more ambitious northstar design direction that would guide the rest of the engagement. They were wanting something modern, colorful, and a little playful to bring more life to the monotony that is compliance software.

Design System

Created and documented the use-case and functionality of a series of components and foundational elements based on the previous conceptual work.
