My Role

Product Designer

Description

Worked to refresh UX patterns for Toyota's newest generation in-car infotainment system.

Context

Toyota's newest 2024 generation infotainment systems had numerous user complaints and they were looking for us to make updates as the flows hadn't been touched since the last generation released in 2021.

Product Design

Leading a revamp of the keyboard search flow, working to make the search experience more valuable to users by surfacing recents, implementing predictive text, and providing the ability to sort and filter results all while designing for the affordances of an in-car experience.

The effort spanned across the music, navigation, phone, and settings domains and required collaborating with the stakeholders and lead designers from each domain.
